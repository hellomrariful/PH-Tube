Discuss the scope of var, let, and const

01 September 2023

In JS, We can declare variables in three way. The way is var, let and const. Let's Discuss the scope of variables.

var: It's can be both reassigned and redeclare.
let: It's allow to reassign but you can't redeclare.
const: It's can't reassigned another value or redeclare.
You shouldn't use var anymore. You should use let or const instead. if you're not going to change the value of a variable, it is good practice to use const.
